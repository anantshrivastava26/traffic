20%

Why Chronological Split?

Random split would cause data leakage — future data leaking into training. Chronological split mimics real deployment: train on past, predict future.

Test Period: 2017-2018

Model was never exposed to 2017-2018 data during training. Evaluation on this unseen period gives true generalization score.
